ABAS Offers

Inspection audit reports and certificates available immediately after the inspection has been completed.

ABAS is a real time system. As the process of bringing buildings into compliance the system tracks and reports on the process. All reports and compliance certificates are made available immediately upon completion which, in most cases, are at the completion of the on site inspection.

ABAS member access allows all clients access to their audit reports and certificates at any time.

ABAS is a fully accessible system. With Member access your compliance reports and data is available at any time. As audits are completed they are immediately accessible

ABAS has Member access to our resource centre.

ABAS makes available a range of information from the legislative requirements to helpful data for managing clients. This resource centre is located on the web site but is only FULLY accessible to Members.

ABAS insures that your buildings are been audited by the due dates, automatically ensuring up-to-date reporting for compliance.

ABAS automatically logs in all due dates for all compliance dead lines. All buildings will be audited and compliance certificates issued by the due dates or remedial action identified.

ABAS provides all inspectors up-to-date data to insure accurate audit process.

ABAS inspectors can access all documentation for all audits from the central data base while undertaking the inspection. This speeds up the process and ensures a more accurate and cost effective inspection.

ABAS includes a photographic record of non-compliant items to ensure clarity of the audit and speedy rectification of non-compliance items.

ABAS systems have a full photographic record of all relevant non-complying issues that arise from the inspection. This photographic record aids in the location and rectification of the non-complying item. Contractors undertaking any rectification are able to more accurately identify the item and provide a more cost effective solution.

ABAS is a paperless system that does not require the retention and filing of documentation.

ABAS stores all the information on all aspects of the auditing process. As the system has Member access the Member is able to access the system at any time to inspect their audits and compliance details.

ABAS Emails all reports certificates and accounts for ease of handling (paper copies are available upon request).

ABAS is a fully on line system that uses the net to conduct all transactions including reporting, auditing and compliance certification. Accounts are also emailed for ease of handling. Complete audit trail of all information transactions.

ABAS retains and maps all correspondence and documentation for all buildings by address. This system will track the progress of the audit and certification of your building.

ABAS is run from a secure and fully backed up remote database.

ABAS is hosted at a remote location and is fully backed up insuring that all data is secure.

ABAS supplies a simplified system to the whole auditing process. It removes unnecessary paper work or the need to maintain paper records. Reports certificates such as annual essential safety measure reports and quarterly door reports are all available as required. Reports happen in real time. As the inspection is complete you can access it and know immediately any remedial work required or compliance achieved.

The process is easily accessible and transparent and the efficiencies generated allows AESG to pass on the benefits to you the customer. You have the confidence to know that the audit is proceeding and comes direct to you.
